FAQs

WHAT ARE YOUR HOURS?
We are open from 9 a.m. to 6 p.m., by appointment, every day of the week.

DO YOU OFFER TOURS?
We are happy to offer tours by appointment.

HOW DO I MAKE A RESERVATION?
Call us at 503-429-0806 or e-mail us.

WHAT ARE CHECK-IN AND CHECK-OUT TIMES?
Dogs can check in and out by appointment anytime between 9 a.m. and 6 p.m., however, should you need to come between 8 a.m. and 7 p.m., we can accommodate you.  Please note that unlike most boarding facilities, we do not set a strict check out time of noon.  We charge by the night, not the hour.

WHERE IS INDIGO RANCH HOMESTYLE BOARDING IN THE COUNTRY  LOCATED AND HOW DO I GET THERE?
We are located at 15640 Airport Way, Vernonia, Oregon 97064.

Click here for directions.

WHAT PAPERWORK DO I NEED TO PROVIDE?
Please provide us with the following forms – don’t worry, it doesn’t take long!

  • Indigo Registration Form (pdf)
  • Liability Form (pdf)
  • Check-In Form (pdf)

Check-In Form is required for each visit.

WHAT ARE YOUR BOARDING REQUIREMENTS?
Owners must provide proof of the following vaccinations:

  • Parvo / Distemper (or antibody titer test)
  • Rabies

All dogs must be treated with an effective form of flea and tick control.

Please present the above listed forms and documentation at check-in, or e-mail them to us beforehand.

WHAT SHOULD I BRING WITH MY DOG?
Please bring food and treats for your dog’s entire stay.  Make sure you provide us with an adequate amount by pre-measuring two meals for every day your dog stays with us. Place it in a food bag or container (include the scoop you generally use to measure it) write your dog’s name and feeding instructions.

No need to send beds, blankets or toys for your dog. We provide all the basics. We don’t even need their leashes (we use slip leads) just their collar and food.

WILL YOU GIVE MY DOG HIS/HER MEDICINE?
Yes. We can administer both non-injectable and injectable medication. Just make sure it is clearly labeled with your dog’s name, exact dosage and time instructions.

WHAT IS INCLUDED IN YOUR DAILY RATE?
Everything.

WHAT IF  MY SMALL DOG IS UNCOMFORTABLE AROUND LARGER DOGS?
We offer an exclusive Tiny Dog Dorm with a private yard for little dogs providing specialized attention for dogs who prefer mixing with their own size.

IS YOUR FACILITY SECURE?
Yes. Indigo Ranch staff lives on-site and is available for your dog 24/7.

Our facility, including all doors and gates, all fences and barriers are inspected daily to insure complete safety and security for your dog.

DO YOU OFFER DAY CARE FOR DOGS?
Indigo Ranch Day Camp provides a full day of fun for your dog!

Drop off dogs in the morning and pick them up, tired and satisfied, in the evening.  Dogs play outside all day, inside when it rains.  Nap-time, treat time and dog TV time in between fun time!

Day Camp rates are $25 for the entire day, $18 per half day.

Requirements of Rabies, Parvo / Distemper and the use of an effective form of flea and tick control apply.

Call 503-429-0806 for information and reservations.

WHAT MAKES INDIGO RANCH HOMESTYLE BOARDING IN THE COUNTRY SPECIAL?

  1. Indigo Ranch offers homestyle in the country dog boarding.  No kennels or 24 hour seclusion.
  2. We offer full-time, 24 / 7 love and care. Everything we do is dedicated to your dog’s comfort, safety and fun!
  3. 100% of the proceeds from Indigo Ranch support the life-saving work of Indigo Rescue, a volunteer based organization that creates and maintains programs to save homeless and abandoned dogs and cats.
