Indigo Ranch is a 16 acre country retreat offering homestyle boarding in the country for dogs. 100% of the proceeds support the lifesaving work of Indigo Rescue, a volunteer based organization that creates and maintains programs to save homeless and abandoned dogs and cats.

Location and Directions

INDIGO RANCH
15640 Airport Way,  Vernonia, Oregon 97064
503-429-0806

MAPQUEST WILL GET YOU LOST!  PLEASE FOLLOW THE DIRECTIONS TO INDIGO RANCH BELOW:

From Portland going West :

  1. Take US 26 West towards Ocean Beaches.
  2. Turn Right onto Nehalem Hwy. (OR – 47)  Go approx. 11.8 miles.
  3. Look for Timber Rd and signs for the Vernonia Golf Club and Vernonia Airport.
  4. Turn Left onto Timber Road East.  Go 1.0 mile.
  5. Turn Right onto Airport Road (gravel). Go 0.3 miles
  6. Bear Left onto Airport Way. Go 0.4 miles (past Airport Park on the left).
  7. Indigo Ranch is on the left 1/8 past the entrance to the city park.

From Oregon Coast going East:

  1. Take US 26 East towards Portland.
  2. Turn Left onto NW Vernonia Road. Go 2.6 miles.
  3. Continue onto Timber Road. Go 6.5 miles.
  4. Turn Left onto Airport Road. Go 0.3 miles.
  5. Bear Left onto Airport Way. Go 0.4 miles.
